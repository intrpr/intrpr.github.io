Cognitive models of interpreting

Readings:
Handout (Cokely)
This handout contains Cokely’s Sociolinguistic Model of Interpretation. Now considered the benchmark of scientific and analytic intepreting models, Cokely’s diagram measures interpreting miscues in a scientific manner and helps interpreters determine where they happen. 20K
Handout (Colonomos)
This handout contains Colonomos’ CRP Pedagogical Model of Interpretation. Colonomos’ work focuses on ascertaining the meaning and intent behind a speaker’s message. This model helps interpreters determine where, in the process, they should be attending to specific skills and efforts. 300K
Handout (McIntire & Sanderson)
This handout contains a comparison of four historical approaches (McIntire & Sanderson, 1993) to interpreting situations. 24K
